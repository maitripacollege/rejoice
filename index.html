Welcome to Rejoice! Rejoice is the Maitripa College Student Portal. Rejoice unifies access to all student services, including access to the appointment and contact center in which students can communicate with their advisor, contact billing and student accounts, and request technical support.

Rejoice also includes access to online classes and class recordings and useful community links, as well as the Technology Support Center, which serves our students as a central repository of information for all of our technological infrastructure. The technology support center includes student resources, policies & guidelines, services & contact information, and status of Maitripa College information systems.

To access Rejoice, a Maitripa College email account is needed. Once you receive from our Registrar access to your Maitripa College email account, click on "Sign In" in the top right hand corner, enter your Maitripa College email address and select "Next", enter the password to access your Maitripa College email account and select "Next".

In the Announcements section on the right hand side of this page, you will find announcements about upcoming public events, recent publications on our website, and important announcements relevant to our degree and CE students.

If you have problems logging into your Rejoice account, please contact technicalservices@maitripa.org
